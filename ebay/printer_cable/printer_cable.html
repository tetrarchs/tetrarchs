Printer cable USB to 25 pin (DB25 F)

Description

Personal sale of a new and unused printer cable. This is a USB male to DB-25 female connector that is most often used to connect non-USB compatible printers to modern PCs or laptops that are not made with a DB-25 port. I have my old HP Laserjet 1100 printer connected up using another one of these cables making it a useful second printer.

This comes with a driver CD, though in my case Windows Vista didn't need any drivers installed in order to recognize my printer was connected.

I have included detailed photographs so you can judge good quality and condition for yourself. Please contact me for any clarifications.
