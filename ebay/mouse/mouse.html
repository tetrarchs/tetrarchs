Logitech TrackMan Marble FX

Description

This is a used Logitech TrackMan Marble FX with PS2 connector, model number T-CJ12 / part number 804251-0000. Clean condition from non-smoking environment. No drivers are included with this auction, but can be downloaded for free from the Logitech website.

It is a classic / funky design with many devoted fans due to its ergonomic design and was much lamented when Logitech stopped manufacture. The large tracker-ball is able to pop out of the socket entirely for cleaning purposes. The ball rotates on small ball bearings inside the socket. This has only had one careful owner as I bought this a few years ago to use with laptops but have rarely used it.

An extract from Logitech's marketing:

Place your hand on TrackMan Marble FX and you'll feel the Marble difference. This is the only finger-operated trackball that lets you move the ball with just your finger, or use your finger and thumb for fine detail work. Logitech patented Marble sensing technology replaces all the mechanical moving parts that record motion with an optical tracking system. A laser-like beam illuminates dots printed on the ball, while a sensor tracks the motion. This gives you precision, reduces wear, and ensures smooth tracking even if the ball gets dirty or scratched. Only TrackMan Marble FX offers you this combination of high performance, low maintenance, and reliability.
