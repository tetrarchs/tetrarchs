Eleven Elephants

Description

For sale are these unusual wooden hand carved and painted tiny multi-coloured elephants from Kandy in Sri Lanka. During the festival in Kandy, elephants are decorated in this highly colourful style.

These elephants are about the size of a 1p piece (see photo) and so small enough to be used as earrings or to hang on a bracelet or necklace.

I have included detailed photographs and product turntable below so you can judge their good quality and condition for yourself. Please contact me for any clarifications.

After the photographs were taken I found another elephant that had managed to escape. Hence there are actually 11 for sale and 10 in the photographs. There are 2 blue, 2 pink, 2 red, 2 green, 2 yellow and 1 lonely white one.

Note that the standard certificate of posting limits compensation to £36; additional insurance should be taken out for items over this value.

For items sold for over £20, I will offer refunds if the item is not as described or arrives non-functional. The buyer should contact me within 7 days of receipt and return the item to me at their cost and email evidence of posting (unless agreed otherwise). Any refund will exclude return postage costs and be contingent on recieving the item back for inspection.

For items under £20 please contact me to see if we can reach an accomodation. I am obviously cautious of fraudulent claims but credible claims for refund and in particular when I am demonstrably at fault will be honoured.
