Eleven Elephants

Description

For sale are these unusual wooden hand carved and painted tiny multi-coloured elephants from Kandy in Sri Lanka. During the festival in Kandy, elephants are decorated in this highly colourful style.

These elephants are about the size of a 1p piece (see photo) and so small enough to be used as earrings or to hang on a bracelet or necklace.

I have included detailed photographs and product turntable below so you can judge their good quality and condition for yourself. Please contact me for any clarifications.

After the photographs were taken I found another elephant that had managed to escape. Hence there are actually 11 for sale and 10 in the photographs. There are 2 blue, 2 pink, 2 red, 2 green, 2 yellow and 1 lonely white one.
