LOVIKEN Dual-control kitchen mixer tap

Description

For sale is a new and unused IKEA Loviken single lever kitchen tap. After a recent kitchen fitting in my house, this was left unused but could not be returned as the original packaging had been thrown away by the plumber.

This comes with the original fittings and installation leaflet as supplied by IKEA. Designed for fitting in a 35mm hole in a max. 70mm thick sink/worktop.
