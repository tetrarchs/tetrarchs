Description

This is a personal sale of a mint condition Royal Horticultural Society Chelsea Flower Show official commemorative plate. The plate is fine bone china and made by Wedgwood in England. The 1983 Chelsea Flower Plate is bordered in 22 carat gold and bears an original design by Rosanne Sanders. This plate is in mint condition and would add grace to any English drawing room or entrance hall.
