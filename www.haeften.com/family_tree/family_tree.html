Purpose
AVH_USB, 28 January 2007 (created 28 January 2007)
  • not tagging
  • tags:
  • about
I have had several contacts randomly from other Van Haeftens, using email search tools, researching their genealogy. The intent of creating this page is to act as a focus for this information. I am particularly interested in building up my library of records and photographs to support the database and ensure the quality of the information.

Since I started this family web page, a book has been published on our family called “The Van Haeften family in India (1735-1948)” by Tom Fuller, which is based on records from the India Office.

Database

You can download a copy of my family tree database (100+ records) in GEDCOM (17k) or Brother's Keeper (PK zipped, 17k) format (last updated August 1999).

The default Family Tree page on this site is the most up to date list.

Brother's Keeper (a widely adopted and free to use genealogy program) can be downloaded from:
  • http://netsonic.fi/~rene/bk Finland Site (recommended)
  • http://www.wf.net/~jyates/bkdwnld.html Another genealogy site to try (USA)

I have had several contacts randomly from other Van Haeftens, using email search tools, researching their genealogy. The intent of creating this page is to act as a focus for this information. I am particularly interested in building up my library of records and photographs to support the database and ensure the quality of the information.\n\nSince I started this family web page, a book has been published on our family called &#8220;The Van Haeften family in India (1735-1948)&#8221; by Tom Fuller, which is based on records from the India Office.\n\n!Database\nYou can download a copy of my family tree database (100+ records) in [[GEDCOM (17k)|http:avh.ged]] or [[Brother's Keeper (PK zipped, 17k)|http:avh.zip]] format (last updated August 1999).\n\nThe default [[Family Tree]] page on this site is the most up to date list.\n\nBrother's Keeper (a widely adopted and free to use genealogy program) can be downloaded from:\n*http://netsonic.fi/~rene/bk Finland Site (recommended)\n*http://www.wf.net/~jyates/bkdwnld.html Another genealogy site to try (USA)
