WEEK FIVE

 

TREATMENT OF SLAVES

 

Sources: Wiedemann, esp. ch. 9; Gardner/Wiedemann ch. 2; suggest also reading at least one play by Aristophanes or Plautus

 

Questions to think about:

How were slaves treated?  What motivated this treatment: e.g. fear, affection, economic or ideological reasons &c?  How did the the treatment of slaves differ from that of the free, and if so, how did these differences alter over time?  How far were slaves excluded or included within the household? To what extent were slaves able to enjoy ‘family’ life, or sexual or religious rôles or freedoms?

 

What protection or rights, if any, were afforded to slaves under either Athenian or Roman law?  How and when did these change, if at all, and for what reasons?  How effective were they?

 

What part did slaves play in the legal process and how was this affected by their slave status; e.g. as litigants, accusers/accused, witnesses?  What role did torture play under Athenian and Roman law?  How was it justified?
