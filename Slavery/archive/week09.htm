Slavery in the Classical World: Week 9

 

VNFREE LABOUR

 

Sources:  Wiedemann ch. 2; Cartledge 1979 Appendix 4; Dillon and Garland Sourcebook 11.27-39; Hauken 1998 esp. nos. 1 and 3; C. Pharr, Theodosian Code (1952), index under coloni

 

Questions to consider:

Look again at the definition of slavery &c from week 1.  What are the main differences between chattel slavery and other forms of unfree labour?  Are they crucial?  In what ways did the Greek/Roman attitude differentiate different forms of ‘unfree’ labour, if at all?  How did they think of the Helots in relation to chattel slavery at Athens?  What are the main similarities and differences between the legal status, economic function, social role, and actual treatment of Helots and chattel slaves?

 

What are the main status distinctions in late antiquity?  How do they differ from the earlier empire?  To what extent is the ‘decline’ of slavery and the ‘development’ of the colonate a function of our evidence?
